About

We are fearless team devoted to the design and development of
future-ready, ultra-efficient computing systems and their applications






  Square One House an innovation house based in Geneva, Switzerland. We invite scientists, artists, engineers and polymaths as fellows to lead and run multidisciplinary research and accelerated programs to develop a technology for targeted applications. Our primary focus areas lie in cognitive sciences, semiconductor design, robotics and artificial general intelligence.

  We uphold research integrity and honesty in our work up to the highest standards. The same is reflected in the quality of our publications. We believe that the highest quality of innovation should be accessible to everyone. That's why we work hard to make our services and technologies accessible to people from all walks of life.
